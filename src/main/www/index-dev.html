Twitter Example

This is an example Twitter application that pretends to be authenticated while you use it. "Login" with your twitter handle in the top right corner, and we'll fetch your profile and make it look like you're logged in. Browse your "timeline" (a collection of HTML5 tweets), and view your latest tweets.
